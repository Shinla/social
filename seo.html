What is SEO marketing?

Search engine optimization (SEO) is a marketing strategy aimed at improving a website's visibility on search engine results pages (SERP). Techniques include keyword optimization, quality content creation, link building, improving site speed, and mobile-friendliness.

How to Start SEO

  1. Find a Keyword: Identify a keyword with substantial search volume.
  2. Understand Search Intent: Comprehend the search intent behind your chosen keyword.
  3. Write Your Page: Develop your page content based on thorough keyword research.
  4. Optimize Meta Title and Description: Craft an optimized meta title and description for your page.
  5. Publish Your Page: Share your optimized page on your website.
  6. Build Links: Actively work on building links to your page or website for increased visibility.
