Types of Facebook Ads

Image Ads

Video Ads

Increasingly popular for businesses. Can run in Facebook News Feeds and stories or appear as ads in longer Facebook videos.

Poll Ads

Mobile-specific format allowing audience participation. Add a two-option poll to an image or video ad.

Carousel Ads

Use up to 10 images or videos to highlight a product or service. Showcase different products within a campaign, product or service features, or display a new update.

Slideshow Ads

Similar to carousel ads, craft different short video ads designed to capture the attention of potential customers. Quick, affordable, and effective.

Collection Ads

Mobile-only ad format. Designed to make it easier for people to view and purchase your products and services from their mobile device.
