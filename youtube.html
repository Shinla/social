Introducing YouTube Ads

Be seen where everyone is watching. Everyday, over 5 billions of videos are watched on YouTube. This presents a huge opportunity to market your business to this huge audience base through YouTube Ads. With YouTube Ads, you discover a cost-effective way to connect with people who are interested in your products or services.

2 Types of YouTube Ads

Bumper Video Ads

Bumper ads are 6 seconds long and unskippable. Bumper ads are great for branding and shouting out your business’ USP. The pricing model for YouTube Bumper Ads is Cost-Per-Thousand impressions (CPM).

TrueView Video Ads

TrueView Ads are usually more than 30 seconds and are skippable after 5 seconds. TrueView Ads are great for driving engagement and getting viewers to take actions. The pricing model for YouTube TrueView Ads is Cost-Per-View (CPV). You only pay for qualified viewers if they choose to watch your video ad for at least 30 seconds.

Only Pay When They Watch Your Video Ad

With YouTube Ads, you only pay when someone watches your video for at least 30 seconds or click on your TruView Ad.

Remarketing Ads

Remarketing ads allow you to show targeted ads to users who have already visited your website. You can set up specific goals, for example users who visited your website but did not fill up a form, and show them remarketing ads created specifically to prompt them to return and complete the form.

Together with Search Ads and machine learning technology, remarketing ads can help you increase conversions and maximize your ads spend.
